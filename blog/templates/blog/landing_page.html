Four rules making pages

Space

Easy rule, that often break: put near elements with similar mean and surround them with space. Space - is powerful for impact.

Grid

Size of lements and distance between they put in harmony — it named grid. If element drop, people can see that.

Fonts

No more than three fonts on site. One - for header, second - for all another texts, third — marking important words. Should be enought.

Colors

Commonly on site two colors: main and accent. Take a good color pair is possible due to the lack of visibility or special tools.
